HOW TO PLAY


Guess the CREWDLE (the right word) in six tries.
Each guess must be a valid five-letter word that is relevant to the OFMD universe. Hit the enter button to submit

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


EXAMPLES

The letter A is in the word but in the wrong spot
