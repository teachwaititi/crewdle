Crewdle

Play it through as a crew!

    HOW TO PLAY


    Guess the the right word in 6 tries:

    1. Type in your guess
    2. Each guess must be a 5 letter word that
    is relevant to the OFMD universe
    3. Hit the enter button to submit

    After each guess, the color of the tiles
    will change to show how close your guess
    was to the word.

    EXAMPLES
    The letter S is in the word and in
    the correct spot

    The letter A is in the word but in
    the wrong spot

    Letters in gray are not in
    the word in any spot
